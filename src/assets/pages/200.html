A11.5 Language focus: Past events
Look at this sentence:
We were eating our meal last night when uncle left.
LAST NIGHT
8.30 p.m.
We were eating our meal (past continuous)
8.00 p.m. 9.00 p.m.
My uncle left (past simple)
We use the past continuous tense when two actions continued for some time together.
Example:
While the deer were grazing, a leopard saw them.

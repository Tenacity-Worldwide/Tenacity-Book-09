B7.7 Language focus: Passive sentences
1 Can you remember the difference between active and passive sentences?
Look at these sentences and identify which are active and which are passive.
1 The governments will build ring roads around the city.
2 Many new skyscrapers will be built.
3 More children will go to secondary school.
4 More money will be spent on the schools.
5 Students will be given computers to help study.
6 Students will learn more languages in school.
2 Below, look at how we can change the first two sentences in exercise 1 into the active or
passive form.
1 Ring roads will be built around the city.
2 People will build many new skyscrapers.
3 Change the other four sentences from exercise 1 into the active or passive. Write them in
your exercise book.
4 Not all active sentences can be expressed in the passive.
Example:
Ali came in, went to bed and slept for eight hours.

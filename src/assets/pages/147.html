A8.8 Speaking: Dialogue in a bank
1 Commission is the money a bank charges to change one currency into another. What other
words or phrases do you know that you would use in a bank?
2 Listen as your teacher reads the following
dialogue; then read the dialogue again in
pairs.
A: Good morning. How can I help you?
B: Good morning. I’d like to change some dollars
into birr, please.
A: How much would you like to change?
B: What is the exchange rate?
A: 12.6 birr to the dollar.
B: OK. I’d like to change $220, please.
A: That will be 2,780 birr and 278 birr
commission.
B: That’s fine.
A: Here you are.
B: Thanks.

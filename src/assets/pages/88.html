A5.3 Speaking: Discussion about AIDS
1 In the listening text we heard one of the myths about HIV / AIDS: that having sex with a
virgin can prevent you from getting the HIV virus. There are many other myths about HIV.
Work in a small group and discuss each of these statements. Decide if you agree or disagree
with each statement and give the reason for your opinion.
1 You can wash the virus away by washing with disinfectant soon after having sex.
2 Abstaining from sex is the only safe way to avoid the virus.
3 Condoms are 100% safe.
4 Drinking alcohol makes it harder for you to say ‘no’ to sex.
5 If someone asks you to have sex with them, it means that they love you.
6 You can have a boyfriend or a girlfriend without having sex with them.

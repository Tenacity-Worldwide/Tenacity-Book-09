B6.11 Study skills: Spelling quiz
1 Learn the following spelling rules for comparative adjectives.
adjectives that end in
● -y, change the -y to i and add –er: early – earlier/ happy – happier
adjectives that end in -
● e, add -r to end of the adjective: nice – nicer/ safe – safer
adjectives that end in a consonant double the last letter:
● big – bigger/ fat – fatter
2 Your teacher will dictate a number of adjectives. Listen, and write the comparative forms in
your exercise book, then exchange your book with your partner to mark your spellings.

B8.6 Writing: Making a summary
A summary is a short version of a longer text. It contains the most important information from the
longer text.
To write a summary, first read and understand the text. Then make notes on the text in your own
●
words.
To make the notes, identify the main point and the supporting points in each paragraph. The
●
supporting points give us more information about the main point.
Finally write your own summary text in full sentences, using the information in your notes.
●
Make brief notes about the winner of the Ethiopian National lottery. Write a summary of
no more than five sentences. You may decide to agree or disagree with the writer’s point of
view. Share your summary with the rest of the class.
